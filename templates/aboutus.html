About Us

Well the site is created by a person who was diagnosed with OCD, he wanted to create a space for people like him. The current mental health scene has a lot of stigma attached to it, this has to be changed, the main attempt of this website is to create a safe space where people suffering from different mental health issues can have access to all the information that they need which will help them get better.

We even have a donate page if you like our effort please support us by donating to this cause.
